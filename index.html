In my free time you'll definitely catch me watching shows like The Good Place, Arcane, and Frieren: Beyond Journey's End. Some of my favorite YouTube chanels are Michael Penn / Math Major, 3Blue1Brown, The Bright Side of Mathematics, and Dr. Trefor Bazett.
